### Tables

- Outer pipes are optional, 3 spaces after headers
- Colons can be used to align columns

|Tables   | Are     | Awesome |   
|---------|:-------:|--------:|
| *col 3 is*|__right-aligned__|$1600|

- Inline markdown allowed as well.

|Row A   |Row B   |Row C   |Row D|
|-------:|:-------|:------:|-----|
|Text A|__Text B__| *Text C*|Text D|
|Text A|__Text B__| *Text C*|Text D|

- without outer pipes
